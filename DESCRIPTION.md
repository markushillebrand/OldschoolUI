# OldschoolUI

**A complete, lightweight UI suite for World of Warcraft: Mists of Pandaria
Classic (5.5.x).** One clean, consistent look across action bars, unit frames,
nameplates, bags and more — fully configurable from a single options panel, with
a colour accent you choose.

OldschoolUI is a modular suite: install it whole, or disable any module you do
not want. Everything is themed around one accent colour, and the entire
interface recolours when you change it.

---

## Highlights

- **One panel for everything.** Every module is configured from the same options
  window, grouped by category, with a per-module enable switch.
- **Your colour, everywhere.** Pick a UI accent colour and the whole suite —
  bars, toggles, sliders, highlights — adopts it.
- **Movable frames.** Unlock with a click (or `/ouimove`) and drag any module
  window where you want it.
- **German included.** Full deDE localisation; English by default.
- **Light footprint.** Modern, focused code — only the Ace3 libraries are
  bundled.

## Modules

### Action Bars
Reskinned main and secondary bars, encounter / extra-action buttons, XP and
reputation bars, flyout skinning, per-bar visibility and combat fade, and a
choice of square or round buttons.

### Unit Frames
Player, target, focus, pet and target-of-target frames with portraits,
class-coloured health, power, and aura tracking with hover tooltips.

### Raid & Party Frames
Clean, compact group frames for parties and raids.

### Resource Bars
Primary and secondary resource display for every spec — combo points, chi, holy
power, shadow orbs, soul shards, burning embers, demonic fury, eclipse and
death-knight runes — plus a cast bar, combat fade, and low-resource alerts.

### Nameplates
Slim, readable nameplates with cast bars, aura tracking, threat colouring, raid
markers and a class-power display on your target's plate.

### Bags
An all-in-one inventory with sidebar categories, item levels, quality borders,
sorting, gold tracking, and pinned / recent items.

### Aura & Buff Reminders
At-a-glance reminders for weapon enchants, flasks and food, raid buffs, and
talent/spell reminders.

### Quality-of-Life
A collection of comfort tweaks that smooth out everyday play.

### Damage Meter
A built-in damage / healing meter.

### Group & Challenge Timer
Live timers for Challenge Mode, Heroic and raid runs, with boss splits and a
per-instance stats history.

### Quest Tracker
A cleaner, repositionable objective tracker.

### Loot & Bonus Roll
Reskinned loot-roll and bonus-roll frames.

### Friends & Chat
Skinned friends list and chat windows.

### Blizzard Skin
Reskins core Blizzard frames (character sheet with the equipment manager,
interactive slots and detailed stats) to match the suite.

---

## Getting started

1. Install and reload.
2. Open the options panel (Escape → OldschoolUI, or the minimap button).
3. Set your accent colour under **Global Settings**, toggle modules on or off,
   and tune each module to taste.
4. Use **Move Frames** (or `/ouimove`) to reposition windows, then lock them.

## Compatibility

- **WoW Classic – Mists of Pandaria 5.5.x** (interface `50504`).
